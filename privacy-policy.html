Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17th, 2025

Welcome to Thunderstruck ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. COLLECTION OF YOUR INFORMATION

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App includes:

Personal Data:

Personally identifiable information, such as your email address, display name, and age, that you voluntarily give to us when you register with the App.

Profile Data:

Photographs and text-based prompts that you voluntarily upload to create your profile ("Sparks"). This includes your prompts.

User-Generated Content:

  • Ratings ("Charges") you give to other users' Sparks.
  • Messages you exchange with your match during a "Thunderstorm."

Financial Data:

We may collect data related to your payment method (e.g., valid credit card number, card brand, expiration date) when you choose to pay the "Commitment Fee" to continue a chat. We use a third-party payment processor (e.g., Stripe, Braintree) and we do not store full financial data on our servers.

2. USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate your anonymous "Sparks" to be displayed to other users.
  • Run our proprietary matching algorithm (the "Scryfall") to find your most compatible match.
  • Facilitate communication between you and your match during a "Thunderstorm."
  • Process payments and refunds.
  • Notify you of updates to the App.

3. DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is primarily used within the App's ecosystem. Your "Sparks" are displayed anonymously to other users for rating. Your displayName and full profile are only revealed to a match after a successful "Thunderstorm" and mutual agreement to continue chatting. We will not share your personal information with any third parties except as necessary for the App's functionality (e.g., payment processors).

4. SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. We use Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage) which provide industry-standard security. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You may at any time review or change the information in your account or terminate your account by:

  • Accessing your account settings and updating your profile.
  • Contacting us using the contact information provided below.

Upon your request to terminate your account, we will deactivate or delete your account and information from our active databases.

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
